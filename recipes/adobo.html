Adobo

Adobo is a popular Filipino dish that involves meat, seafood, or vegetables marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and black peppercorns, which is browned in oil, and simmered in the marinade. It has a strong, savory flavor that is both sweet and salty. It is often served with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 pound chicken thighs
  • 1 pound pork belly, cubed
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup water
