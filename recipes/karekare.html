Kare-Kare

Kare-Kare is a popular Filipino dish that involves oxtail, tripe, and vegetables stewed in a rich peanut sauce. It is often served with bagoong (fermented shrimp paste) and rice.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup ground toasted rice
  • 1/2 cup annatto seeds
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup cooked oxtail
  • 1/2 cup cooked tripe
  • 1/2 cup cooked string beans
  • 1/2 cup cooked eggplant
  • 1/2 cup cooked banana heart
  • 1/2 cup cooked bok choy
